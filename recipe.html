Recipe of Mango Pomelo Sago Dessert

Introduction

Hi everyone, thanks for coming to this website. Today, I am going to show you how to make a Hong Kong style dessert called “Mango Pomelo Sago”. If you are a mango lover, YEAH.. you have come to a right place. Lalalalala… (singing) Sorry that I may be just too excited to show you this recipe. If you are not Asian, you probably have not heard that before because it is not too popular in America or Europe. Either you have heard this dessert or not, today it is a chance to make one for yourself. Mango Pomelo Sago is mainly made of mango, tapioca, and grapefruit. The tapioca should be found in Asian market. It should be a bag of many small balls in white color. It is one of the healthy desserts in Hong Kong because mango and grapefruit contain a variety of vitamin and other rich nutrients. OK!! Let's get started it.

Ingredients: (serving for 5-6 people)

  • Mango 500g
  • milk 300ml
  • coconut milk 300ml
  • white tapioca 50g
  • 1 grapefruit (remove the skin and stripping out the flesh)
  • water 100ml
  • crystal sugar 60-80g (or up to your flavor)

Tools:

  • A blender
  • A bowl
  • A spoon
  • A pot

Steps:

  1. Cook the white tapiocas until they turn their color into transparent (Note that the center of each tapioca should be white color). While boiling the tapioca, make sure you keep stirring them in circles. Otherwise, they will stick all together. Then drain the water and cool them down for a while.
  2. Boling the 100ml with crystal sugar until the crystal sugar is melted
  3. Now go ahead and divide the mango 500g into 200g and 300g.
  4. Grab 300g of mango, milk, coconut milk, and the sugar water (step 2) into a blender until all ingredients become liquid
  5. Chop the another mango 200g into small pieces
  6. Lastly, put all the ingredients together in a large bowl and put it in refrigerator for 3-4 hours
  7. Ready for party :)

Note: If your party have less than 4 people, you could cut all the ingredients in a half.
